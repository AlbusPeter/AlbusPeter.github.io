Hi! I'm a Master student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California, San Diego. I currently work in SVCL advised by Prof. Nuno Vasconcelos and co-advised by Prof. Bhaskar D. Rao. Before that I was a Bachelor student at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, where I worked with Prof. Shuaicheng Liu. My research interests include: image and video processing, computer vision, and applications on medical imaging. I am also highly interested in human vision system. I will continue my researches as a PhD student in UC Berkeley Vision Science this Fall.
